Voice of Minorities

Introduction:




It is with great pleasure that we are going to organize Voice of Minorities Season 5 (Singing Competition) in the history of Pakistan to search best talent in singing field from Minorities. I see this as a lifetime changing opportunity for minority communities where we will get an exposure to different cultures, which will surely benefit the cultural activities and create milestone in the revival of culture. It will prove great platform for minorities (Hindu, Sikh, Christian, Kalash, Bahá’í) to show their talent and enthusiasm for beloved Country. Through this activity the Youth from Minorities will demonstrate their confidence and build experience.


Objectives:


  • To create peace & Interfaith Harmony
  • Revival of Religious Scripts through Singing
  • Minorities Youth Empowerment



Goal: The project goal is to bring young talent from religious spots in KPK to the international market through their magical voices.
