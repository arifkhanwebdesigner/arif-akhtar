Franchise Call



SUBHASH Educational Complex and Consultancy Services is currently extending an invitation for franchise opportunities in key locations, including Peshawar, Mardan, Nowshera, Swat, Abbottabad, Chitral, Kohat, and Bannu. With an extensive background in the training and development sector, our organization boasts a proven track record of successful projects in collaboration with both national and international NGOs. As we embark on this franchise expansion, we are seeking dedicated and driven partners who share our commitment to educational excellence. This strategic initiative aims to bring our high-quality capacity building training, vocational training, community based enterprise development to a broader audience, fostering educational growth and empowerment in these regions. Prospective franchisees can expect to leverage our experience, comprehensive curriculum, and support system to establish thriving educational centers in their respective communities.

SUBHASH Educational Complex/Consultancy (SEC) is the ultimate career spot offering a variety of career-oriented programs and opportunities in Peshawar.
